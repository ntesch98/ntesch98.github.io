    I love reading. I grew up an avid reader, but like most people I stopped reading as much in high school and college. Two years ago during summer break, I found myself with a lot of extra time on my hands and started reading again. Ever since, I try to read whenever I have the chance. I have read almost fifty books in 2020! Here is a list of my five top favorite books of all time.

    1. The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo by Taylor Jenkins Reid
    2. Gone Girl by Gillian Flynn
    3. The Secret Life of Bees by Sue Monk Kidd
    4. The Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins
    5. A Man Called Ove by Fredrik Backman
